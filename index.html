Nablus, in the West Bank of Palestine, is a city renowned for its rich history and vibrant culture. Its ancient architecture and bustling markets tell tales of resilience and community spirit.

Famous for its olive oil and soap production, Nablus remains a vibrant hub amidst a complex political landscape.

With winding alleys and iconic landmarks like Nablus Castle, the city continues to embody the essence of Palestinian heritage
